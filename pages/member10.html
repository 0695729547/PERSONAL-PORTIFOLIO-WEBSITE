Project Two

The Artisan Market is a specialized e-commerce platform designed to connect local craftspeople with customers seeking unique, handmade goods. Built using HTML5 and CSS3 to create a visually appealing, gallery-style storefront and JavaScript to manage interactive features like product filtering and shopping cart logic, the project serves as a digital bridge for creators who lack traditional retail space.
